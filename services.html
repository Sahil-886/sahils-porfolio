FAQ

Common questions

Answers to the things clients usually ask before starting a project.

How long does a website take?
A standard 4–5 page business website typically takes about 7 days from the day you share your content (text, photos, logo). More complex projects like web apps or mobile apps are scoped individually — I'll give you a clear timeline before we start.
What do you need from me to start?
Just the basics: your business name, what you do, any photos or logos you have, and a rough idea of what you want the site to achieve (bookings, enquiries, showcase, etc.). I'll guide you through everything else — you don't need to have it all figured out.
Do you provide hosting and domain?
I'll help you set up hosting and a domain name, and I can recommend affordable, reliable options. The hosting and domain cost is separate (usually ₹200–500/month depending on the provider) and paid directly to the host — so you always own your site.
What are the payment terms?
50% advance to start, and the remaining 50% on delivery — once you've reviewed and approved everything. No hidden fees, no surprise charges. For custom projects, we can discuss milestone-based payments if needed.
Do you offer support after launch?
Yes. The Growth package includes 1 month of free changes after launch. After that, I offer an optional maintenance plan at ₹500/month for updates, backups, and small changes. For one-off fixes, just reach out — I'm always happy to help.
