About

Builder first. Everything else second.

All of this while pursuing my B.Tech in Information Technology at Pimpri Chinchwad University, Pune (expected Dec 2027), with a Professional Certificate in AI & Data Science from upGrad.

I've also written and published a full-length psychology book, The Hidden You (Sahil Publications), available on Amazon — because understanding people is half of building things they love.

Off the keyboard: football, trekking, music, and a structured fitness transformation I take as seriously as my code.
